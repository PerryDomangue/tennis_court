  • Policies


Cookies

  •  To ensure the best experience with our web application, please make sure that your browser has "cookies" enabled. Cookies help our application remember your preferences and previous actions, such as your reservation search results or the selected days for the schedule. This means that when you refresh or reload a page, you won't need to start your search from scratch.

  •  These web browser cookies allow the server to discern "who said what" bascially. All cookie data is stored for only a matter of hours. Thank you for your understanding.



IP Addresses

  •  Our service actively monitors IP addresses (user networks) to mitigate malicious software bots from misusing service. Currently the IP addresses are only associated with a timestamp.

  •  Once the IP address are stored, they will get automatically deleted in a matter of hours; except for contact-us messages. Users that exceed the unspecified activity-limit will be blocked from the website for a unspecified period of time.



Cancellations

  •  Cancelling is free, we just ask that you do it as soon possible so that others my use that reservation time. The policy is to be considerate and fair to others.

  •  Failing to adhere to this policy repeatedly could result in someone contacting us to complain about misuse of a registry. If multiple people start to complain, then the admin will investigate and any bad-actors will be blocked from the website for a extended period of time.



Empty Sub-locations

  •  If the courts are reserved but they are not being used and the duration of the reservation is half way over, then that court is open for walk-ins, for the remainder of the timeslot.



User Verification

  •  Users must be authenticated before they can reserve, join, or cancel a game. This web application uses a simple method to increase security and streamline the registry process. After users make their final selection, they are redirected to a page with a Turing Test to confirm they are not automated bots.

  •  This method enhances security by avoiding the need to store email addresses on the server, thus eliminating the risk of email-related breaches. It also minimizes the overall hassle of the process, as users do not need to provide and re-enter email addresses, or search through their emails to find a verification code.



Over-booking

  •  Users are not allowed to exceed the reservation limit for any sub-location. Each sub-location has a separate daily maximum reservation limit, which is typically 1-3 per day, and a separate daily maximum joining limit, which is typically 3-5 per day; but they vary for each sub-location.

  •  If you reach the limit for a specific sub-location and wish to make additional reservations for the same park on the same day, please select a different sub-location. You can always unreserve or unjoin from previous games to offset that limit per day.



Tennis vs. Pickle Ball

  •  Pickle ball users should schedule there reservation on a Tennis Court Registry, unless that park has a designated Pickle Ball Court(s), or if local laws say otherwise.
